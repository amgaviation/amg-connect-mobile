## Brand And Content Notes

The site uses a dark premium aviation direction, the approved white AMG logo, operational copy, and careful disclaimers around review and acceptance.

AMG Connect is described as a role-based support view for approved owners, crews, partners, and administrators, covering support requests, aircraft profiles, crew review, documents, quotes/invoices, and status updates.

## Public Content Rules Relevant To Mobile

- Do not imply support is accepted at form submission.
- Keep aircraft status, crew availability, owner/operator approval, operating conditions, support-scope review, and final acceptance visible in the product language.
- Avoid guaranteed crew availability.
- Avoid fake live tracking.
- Keep portal access role-scoped and approval-based.

## Legal/Disclaimer Language To Preserve

The website states that AMG support requests are subject to aircraft status, crew availability, owner/operator approval, operating conditions, support-scope review, and final acceptance. Mobile copy should preserve this stance.
